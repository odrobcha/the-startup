A trip to the Moon

How can visitors get to the Moon? By rocket, of course. MoonWalk will keep you informed about how rockets are launched from Earth, how they fly through space and how they land on the Moon and back. And before the trip you will receive a comprehensive training on all these processes. MoonWalk company helps everyone to make their dreams come true – “to have a lunch” at the Moon

What awaits you?

Our company propose astronaut training program after which you can make a Round Way Trip to the Moon. Our trained and open-minded astronauts will accompany you during all the trip. What awaits you? You will become a part of spaceship team and enjoy beautiful views of the Moon as well as the Earth. You will gain zero-gravity experience and enjoy it. Make your own steps on the Moon.
